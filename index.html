In 2026, I started over deliberately. Studying for the Claude Certified Architect (CCA) Foundations exam, Anthropic's first official AI architecture certification.

But I did not just study quietly. I built a CCA study app, now available at /architect-zero/. I launched a YouTube learning series to teach every lesson the day I learned it. I brought my family - a 13-year-old, a 17-year-old, and a non-IT adult - along for the ride.

ARCHITECT ZERO
ON YOUR HOME SCREEN
iOS · Safari

Install on iPhone or iPad

  1. Open venkata.info/architect-zero/ in Safari
  2. Tap the Share button (square with arrow up)
  3. Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen
  4. Tap Add in the top right

Works offline once installed. Sync stays live when online.

Android · Chrome

Install on Android

  1. Open venkata.info/architect-zero/ in Chrome
  2. Tap the menu (⋮) in the top right
  3. Tap Install app (or Add to Home Screen)
  4. Confirm Install

Native-feeling app icon. Push notifications when enabled.

Architect Zero is a Progressive Web App — no App Store / Play Store download required. Native iOS & Android wrappers may follow if there's demand.
